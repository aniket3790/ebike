Leading the eBike Revolution

Blix’s goal is to inspire people to live a more fun and healthier lifestyle by creating compelling ebikes that combine style, utility, and performance at an affordable price point. Founded in Santa Cruz, CA in 2014, Blix is one of the fastest growing, direct to consumer, ebike brands in the U.S. Blix offers innovative e-bike models including city, cruiser, cargo and folding ebikes. Since we designed and built our first e-bike, thousands of folks have ditched their cars for a more enjoyable ride on the most stylish, comfortable e-bike around.
